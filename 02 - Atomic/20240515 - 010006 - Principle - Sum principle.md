> [!NOTE] Sum principle
> With infinite disjoint sets. 
> 
> **IF:** Any pair of sets is **finite and pairwise disjoint** , namely: $A_i \cap A_j \not = \emptyset : \forall i\not = j$
> 
> **THEN:** 
> $$
> |A_1 \cup A_2 \cup ...\cup A_m| = |A_1| + |A_2| + ... + |A_m| = \sum^{m}_{j = 1}|A_j|
> $$
