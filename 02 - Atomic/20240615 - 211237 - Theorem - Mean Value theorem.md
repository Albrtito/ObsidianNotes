# Mean value theorem: 

> [!NOTE] Theorem: Mean value 
> If f is continuous on [a,b] and differentiable on (a,b), then there is some c such: 
> $$
> c \in (a,b)
> $$
> $$
> \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a} = f'(c)
> $$
>
