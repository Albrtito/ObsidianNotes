# Continuity theorems: 
This note collects theorems related to continuity, from basic properties to theorems such as Bolzano’s: 


> [!NOTE] Composition 
> If g is continuous at $x_0$ and f is continuous at $g(x_0)$ then: 
> $$
> f \circ g
> $$ 
> Is **continuous at $x_0$**
